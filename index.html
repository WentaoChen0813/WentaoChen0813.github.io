About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at USTC, working with Prof. Zilei Wang. I am also in the joint training program at CASIA, supervised by Academician Tieniu Tan, Prof. Liang Wang, and Associate Prof. Zhang Zhang. I received my B.S. degree from Department of Automation of USTC in 2017.

My current research interests lie in the few-shot image classification, self-supervised representation learning and multi-modal learning.
